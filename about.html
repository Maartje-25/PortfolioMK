About

My name is Maartje Kreijtz. I am a dedicated and inquisitve ready to take on projects with my enthusiasm.

View My Works

Right now, I'm fully immersed in my Creative Business studies at Breda University of Applied Sciences. After trying out two other programs and taking two gap years, I have finally found a study I truly enjoy. I love the creative freedom and the dynamic, hands-on education I get here. At school, I naturally gravitate toward leadership roles in group projects. Through this journey, I have discovered a real passion for production, and I’m actively chasing every opportunity to grow and gain experience in that field.

I am a firm believer in equal rights for everyone and in the importance of lifelong learning. I also see sustainability as a key value, and truly believe that we all share the responsibility to care for our planet and contribute to a more sustainable future.

One of my greatest passions is traveling and discovering new cultures. I also love spending time outdoors and staying active, whether it’s working out, hiking, or sailing with friends. I am often dubbed the content creator of my friend group, always ready to capture the moment. Creating videos is something I genuinely enjoy and feel passionate about.
