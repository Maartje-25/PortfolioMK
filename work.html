Live TV show

[Still ongoing] In this project we are making a live TV Show from scrath. Some of the elements for this would be, set design, graphic design, script and story writing, and finding talent. My show, called 'THE SHOW' will be all about performance art, showing creativity, dedication and originality that all belongs to being a performer.

As executive producer for this Show, I oversee a team of 20 students, plan, file and organize all of the TV Show and be the center of communication. Besides this I am a big part of the creative concept of the show, making sure the message stays throughout the whole TV Show.

Student Ambassador

As a part time job I work as a Student Ambassador for my studyprogram Creative business. As a student ambassador go to fairs and highschools to talk about my school and program, or I help on campus with campus tours, voluntary work and helping prospective students make the right study choices. I also help with content for the socials when promoting a special events, as well as day in the lives of a student.

Skills that have improved with this job is communication, public speaking, and networking.
