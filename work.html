Ethics Podcast

Finished Year 2, 2025

An important part of our study program at Creative Business is learning about ethical legislation and laws within the media field, as well as being able to implement this in our own projects. As a part of this course, we had to make a podcast about a media related topic and debate the ethical laws with it.

We made a podcast with the Ethical question: “Should filmmakers prioritise accuracy over storytelling, or is it acceptable to alter history for entertainment?” For this we invited English historian Patrick Farelly to represent the historical side, as me and my team member represented the filmmakers' side of this debate.
